Top-rated Manga more like top rated yuri

Kimi to Tsuzuru Utakata

Status: Finished. - Score: 10/10

Reader's comment:

The first manga of the entry, and also one of the final bosses of doomed yuri. Thinking about it now and then, I really don't know how the author managed to use the most predictable plot and made it 10 times more emotional and captivated me as a reader. I wholeheartedly recommend this to every yuri specimen, seriously.

Akatsuki no Yona

Status: Currently Reading. - Score: 9/10

Reader's comment:

One of the most complex manga I've ever read. The story and the flow might not keep you excited at times, and there are some weak arcs (the Kai arc), yet it has some of the most captivating moments between main characters, arch-nemesis, and mythical creatures alike.

Watashi wo Tabetai, Hitodenashi

Status: Currently Reading. - Score: 9.5/10

Reader's comment:

The story contributes to human trauma and coming to terms with themselves. It's overly dramatic, but it's well-executed with stunning frames and emotional expressions. It's worth reading if you're into horror and psychological aspects, alongside some whimsy Slice of Life breather chapters.

Kishuku Gakkou no Juliet

Status: Finished. - Score: 9.5/10

Reader's comment:

A story full of joy and dramatic between the couple who live in separate nations holding different views towards each other with awesome character development and impeccable fanservices. Romeo to me is the realest and most relatable sigma male character, next to Hak Son.

Kaguya-sama wa Kokurasetai: Tensaitachi no Renai Zunousen

Status: Finished. - Score: 9.8/10

Reader's comment:

This manga dragged me in a timeline full of events that shaped me into the man I am today. The reason I was able to make new friends without succumbing to total loneliness was because of all the joys the Discord server has brought, and I can't thank them enough. Oh, and, this manga is good, btw. The later arcs aren't as memorable as the Christmas arc but still ends on a high note.
